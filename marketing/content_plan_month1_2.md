## Image Production Notes

### Image Types

1. **Hero images** — Main article images, should be bright and informative, 1200x630px recommended for social sharing
2. **Illustrations** — Diagrams, charts, tables (Figma/Canva). Use consistent brand colors (dark blue #1A1A2E, green, orange, teal)
3. **Live photos** — Real photographs of plants, equipment, setups

### Photo Guidelines

- All photos should be well-lit
- Avoid showing faces, locations, identifying information
- Focus on plants and equipment
- Can use stock photos with commercial usage rights
- Recommended sources: Unsplash, Pexels, or original photography

### File Naming Convention

```
article-slug_image-type_number.jpg

Examples:
perpetual-harvest-guide_hero.jpg
perpetual-harvest-guide_illustration_1.jpg
perpetual-harvest-guide_live_1.jpg
```

### Alt Text Format

Use the SEO keywords as alt text, formatted naturally:
- Good: "Two tent grow setup showing veg and flower tents side by side"
- Bad: "two tent setup, veg flower tents, dual tent grow room"
